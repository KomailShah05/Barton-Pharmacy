Travel Clinic Services

  • Home
  • Travel Clinic Services

We make your travel vaccination process simple. When it comes to travel, you want to enjoy it – not worry about your health.

If you are planning to travel and want prevention from harmful diseases, then Westlake Pharmacy is the right place for you.

We provide a full range of services from Travel Health advice and tips, to Travel Vaccinations. We also specialize in Malaria advice and prevention medication as well as Hajj and Umrah pre-travel advice and vaccinations.

Our Team of Clinical Professionals
  • Advice on all your travel health needs.
  • Give you information about compulsory vaccinations you may need for your destination and others which are recommended so you can make an informed choice.
  • Administer a comprehensive range of vaccines.
  • Treat you with dignity and compassion
Travel Risk Assessment

A comprehensive risk assessment should be undertaken for any person who is travelling. Consider all travel-related risks, prevention advice and provisions for the traveller, such as insect bite avoidance, immunisations, and malaria prophylaxis. You’ll need to contact us to arrange this, ideally 6 to 8 weeks before you leave. If your trip is sooner, contact us anyway as we may still be able to help and could provide vital health information.
